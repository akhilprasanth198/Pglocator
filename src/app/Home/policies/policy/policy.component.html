Guest Policies

  • The primary guest must be at least 18 years of age to be able to check in to the property.
  • It is mandatory for guests to present valid photo identification at the time of check-in. The identification proofs accepted are Driving License, Voter ID Card, Photo PAN card, Aadhar card, and Passport.
  • Guests shall be liable for any damage, except normal wear and tear to Property assets, and shall maintain hygiene and cleanliness.
  • Hosts reserve the right to take action against any guest deemed to have inappropriate behavior after proper investigation.
  • Guests cannot cancel bookings on the check-in date onwards and no refund will be entertained in this case.
  • In some properties, unmarried/unrelated couples may not be allowed to check-in. Please confirm with the property before booking.
  • Some properties may deny entry of visitors to rooms. Please confirm with the property before inviting visitors into the rooms.

Cancellation/Refund Process

  • Guest can cancel the booking any time till one day prior to the check-in date. After 23:59 hrs on the check-in date, the booking is auto-cancelled if the guest doesn't show.
  • Cancelled 3 days prior to the check-in date: 100% refund.
  • Cancelled within 3 days to 1 day prior: 70% refund.
  • No cancellations allowed on or after the check-in date.

Booking Extension Process

  • Booking extension is allowed, but host confirmation is mandatory.
